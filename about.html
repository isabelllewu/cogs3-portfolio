About Me

Nice to
meet you.

I'm Isabelle Wu, an aspiring UI/UX designer with a passion for creating innovative designs that improve the world around us.

Currently, I'm a student at UC San Diego pursuing a degree in Cognitive Science specializaing in Machine Learning and minoring in Design. Through my studies and personal projects, I aim to develop user-centered designs that enhance user experiences and solve real-world problems.

I'm an analytical designer at heart — UI/UX gave me exactly what I was looking for: a way to merge creativity with problem-solving. I've joined a community of designers at UC San Diego, leading workshops and planning industry events to bridge the gap between industry and students.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy exploring new places, baking, trying out different cuisines, and spending time with friends and family.

Values

What drives my work

Design with intention. Every decision should serve a purpose — for the user, the product, and the world it lives in.

Eager to learn. Design is always evolving, and so am I. I seek out new perspectives, tools, and challenges constantly.

Clear communication. Great design is only as strong as the thinking behind it — and how well that thinking is shared.
